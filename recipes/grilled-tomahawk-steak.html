Grilled Tomahawk Steak

Recipe by Ashley Elizabeth, recipe and photo sourced from https://www.allrecipes.com/grilled-tomahawk-steak-recipe-7508957

Description

This grilled tomahawk steak recipe is a showstopper, ideal for sharing with friends and family. This reverse-seared steak is cooked to perfection with an unctuous compound butter filled with shallots, garlic, and chives.

I like to use a Bluetooth-enabled meat thermometer to help me achieve the perfect medium-rare internal temperature.

Ingredients

Steak Rub and Steak:

  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon montreal steak seasoning
  • 2 teaspons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 (2 3/4 pound) tomahawk steak (3 inch thick bone-in ribeye)

Compound Butter:

  • 1/4 cup salted butter, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon shallot, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons reserved steak rub
