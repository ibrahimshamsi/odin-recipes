Oven Scrambled Eggs

Recipe and photo sourced from https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/57459/oven-scrambled-eggs/

Description

These baked scrambled eggs are light and fluffy and are a snap to put together for a big crowd.

I usually make two pans for our Christmas brunch, and I never have many leftovers!

9x13 inch oven ready glass dish or similar required
